Profile

Founder with enterprise delivery depth

Ola Dopemu has built and led governance-driven assurance functions that improve execution, strengthen operational resilience, and support large-scale transformation across cloud, legacy, ERP, and bespoke technology estates.

Career experience includes work with IBM UK, the Royal Air Force, Citibank, Lloyds Bank Commercial Services, ECGD, Norsk Hydro Agri, and the British Standards Institution, with particular depth in enterprise delivery, regulatory assurance, automation transformation, and operational readiness.

The founder proposition combines technical credibility, commercial thinking, disciplined governance, and a practical view of how well-designed digital platforms can create sustainable value in underserved and high-friction markets.
